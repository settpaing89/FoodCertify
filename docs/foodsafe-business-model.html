⚙️ App Features

What FoodSafe Does

Every feature mapped to a pricing tier — Free, Premium, or Enterprise.

📷
Barcode Scanner
Scan real product barcodes. Live camera with animated scan line, torch support, and instant lookup from 3M+ products via Open Food Facts.
Free · 10/mo  |  Premium · Unlimited
🛡️
Safety Rating Engine
Analyzes every ingredient against your conditions. Returns SAFE / CAUTION / AVOID verdict with full explanations for each flagged item.
All Tiers · Core Feature
🩺
Health Conditions
Diabetes, Gluten Allergy, Peanut Allergy, Vegan, Vegetarian. Checks 60+ flagged ingredients per condition with clinical-grade explanations.
Free · 2 conditions  |  Premium · All 5
🥗
Dietary Preferences
Configure personal dietary preferences beyond conditions — low-sodium, low-carb, halal, kosher, lactose-free. Custom ingredient blacklists.
Premium · Full Config
🚨
Health Condition Alerts
Real-time push notifications when a scanned product is dangerous. Weekly digest of risky products in your area. Emergency alert for severe allergens.
Premium · Real-time Alerts
💡
Smart Recommendations
When a product is AVOID, suggest 3 safer alternatives from the same category that match your conditions. Powered by ingredient similarity engine.
Premium · AI-Powered
🕐
Scan History
Persistent log of every product you've scanned, with rating, date, brand, and flagged ingredient count. Export as PDF for your dietitian.
Free · Last 5  |  Premium · Unlimited
📝
Manual Ingredient Entry
Paste any ingredient list for analysis — for products not in the database, homemade food, or restaurant menus. Works without a barcode.
All Tiers · Always Free
📚
Ingredient Education
Expandable articles explaining why each ingredient is flagged — glycemic index, hidden gluten sources, peanut cross-contamination, non-vegan additives.
Free · Basic  |  Premium · Full Library
👨‍👩‍👧
Family Profiles
Create separate profiles for each family member with their own conditions and dietary preferences. Scan on behalf of any profile.
Premium · Up to 5 Profiles
📊
Nutrition Panel
Full nutrition facts per 100g, Nutri-Score grade (A–E), NOVA processing group, allergen declarations, and serving size info from Open Food Facts.
All Tiers · Included
🔍
Product Search
Search 3M+ products by name or brand without scanning. Browse results and analyze any product directly from the search list.
All Tiers · Included

💡 Key Insight

The freemium model alone can reach $24,000/month with 100,000 users at an 8% conversion rate. That's achievable within 12–18 months with consistent ASO and social content.

B2B is the highest ROI per effort once you have a working product — one hospital contract at $499/month equals 167 individual subscribers.
